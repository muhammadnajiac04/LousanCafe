Who We Are

THE CHUGGY FOODS STORY

Chuggy Foods LLP was born from a simple but powerful belief — that great fried chicken can bring people together. Founded with a heart full of passion and a kitchen full of secrets, we set out to serve the crispiest, juiciest, most flavour-packed chicken the region has ever tasted.

From our very first outlet, we made a promise: only fresh, locally sourced chicken, marinated in our signature blend of 11 herbs and spices, cooked to golden perfection for every single order. No shortcuts. No compromises.

Today, Chuggy Foods is more than just a restaurant — it's a community. A place where families come to celebrate, friends come to unwind, and food lovers come to experience something truly special. We're proud of every bite we serve and every smile we create.
